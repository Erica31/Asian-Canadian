Vicky Sunohara was born in Scarborough, Ontario, on May 18, 1970. She is an Asian Canadian of Japanese and Ukranian heritage. Sunohara graduated from University of Toronto with a Bachelor of Physical and Health Education degree. Her retirement from international ice hockey in 2008 was followed by twin boys in 2009.

Post retirement, Sunohara shifted her focus more towards coaching. She first served as an assistant coach at the Canadian woman's national under-18 and under-19 evaluation camps. Sunohara was also the Director of womans hockey at The Hill Academy in Vanghan, Ontario for 2 years. Sunohara was named by U sports (the governing body of university sport in Canada), the 2019-20 National Women's Ice Hockey Coach of the Year. Sunohara is still currently the head coach of the Varsity Blues women's ice hockey team of University of Toronto, and also a coach at the minor hockey level with her twin boys.
